Bearspaw Calgary Real Estate and Homes for Sale

Bearspaw defines what rural living is all about while ensuring that urban amenities are accessible conveniently. Life in a rural setting is always going to be peaceful, and so it is when you put down roots in this charming area.

#idx-c-bearspaw#

Bearspaw Real Estate — a peaceful rural community in NW Calgary.

Located in northwest Calgary, just outside the city limits, Bearspaw is a rural community in Rocky View County, named aptly for its incredible views of the Rockies. Bearspaw is to the east of Glendale (also within Rocky View County) and west of Cochrane along Highway 1A.

Today, Bearspaw is viewed as an exclusive community of luxurious homes whose residents wake up to gorgeous views of the mountains and rolling countryside.

Bearspaw is located 30 minutes from downtown Calgary. It is served by schools, a shopping and social hub, sports facilities, parks and of course, the Bow River. The combination of countryside charm and convenient access to urban amenities makes the area a desirable place to live. The homes are on the expensive side, but there's a good mix of builders to choose from.

Amenities in Bearspaw — Live, Work, & Play

Bearspaw offers an abundance of recreation, leisure and shopping. You're never far from a golf course, movie theatre, restaurant or sports facility.

Golf —

Love golfing? You're minutes away from three excellent golf clubs.

  • Lynx Ridge Golf Club

    Tee off at the challenging and immaculately kept 18-hole golf course. Take in the scenic views of mountains and stately homes. Shop at the pro shop, practice your swing at the public driving range, and fuel up at the terrace restaurant.

  • Bearspaw Golf Club

    Fancy playing at one of Alberta's top courses? The 18-hold private golf course at Bearspaw Golf Club is set in a Bill Newis design and covers 6868 yards, with water features, subtle elevation changes and tree-lined fairways. It is challenging enough for regular players, but more suited for beginners and social golfers.

  • Springbank Links Golf Club

    The beautiful 18-hole golf course at Springbank Links weaves through steep valleys and rolling terrain features two lakes and two ponds, and a striking sandstone waterfall. A highlight of the golf club is the Gallery Pub and Restaurant, which offers Calgary's largest selection of craft beers on tap of any course, and handmade foods with herbs and spices from their on-site garden. You can catch stunning views of the sunset over the Rockies from the patio.

Shopping —

Bearspaw Lifestyle Centre is the community area for events, programs, pop-up markets and farmers' markets. You can shop for everything, from vegetables, bison meat and jewellery to hand-crafted candles, perogies and woodworking items. Groceries and other services can be found in the nearby Rocky Ridge shopping centre.

Recreation —

Bearspaw Lifestyle Centre is also the social hub of residents, offering sports activities like baseball, soccer and golf, movies matinees, and Lunch & Learn events. It features a popular Wiggle & Giggle children's play zone.

Bearspaw is north of the Bow River, whose waters, while cold for swimming, are great for boating, kayaking, rafting, canoeing and private or guided tours.

Parks and Nature Preserves —

Parks are the heart of a neighbourhood, serving as a gathering space, facilitating sports and recreation, and are especially beneficial for kids and youth as they promote healthy socializing and a sense of connection as well as community.

Bearspaw has a number of excellent walking paths and hiking trails some of the most popular nearby escapes include:

  • Glenbow Ranch Day park
  • Michael’s Creek
  • Bearspaw Trail

Interesting Facts about Bearspaw

Schools In or Near Bearspaw

Bearspaw school zone falls under Rocky View Schools, the public authority serving students to the west, north and east of Calgary. Families have a choice of public, private and Catholic schools.

Public Schools

  • Bearspaw Elementary School
  • Cochrane High School

Private Schools

  • Renert School
  • Edge School

Catholic Schools

  • St. Luke School
  • St. Margaret School
  • Madeleine d'Houet School

Demographics

As of 2018, Bearspaw had a population of 5,567. More demographic information on this exclusive community is lacking, and will be updated as soon as it becomes available.

Housing

Bearspaw has a mix of old and new developments. The older properties are on vast acreages. You can choose from different lot sizes, but 2-acre parcels of land dominate. Detached and semi-detached homes make up most of the housing styles here. You have a lot of flexibility to build spacious custom homes.

Subdivision Areas

Bearspaw has multiple planned subdivisions:

  • Lynx Ridge Homes

    The small acreage community of Lynx Ridge is home to the Lynx Ridge Golf Course, which winds through the area. The golf course takes up most of the subdivision's green space. The community is bordered to the south by the Bow River. Homes here enjoy stunning views of the Rocky Mountains and sparkling ponds.

  • Church Ranches Estate

    This subdivision is a fine example of rural living. Its landscape is defined by rolling hills. Before the settlement of Canada, coyotes roamed and wild buffaloes grazed here. Today, wildlife peacefully coexists with residents, albeit in park areas. Animal visitors here include bears, coyotes, moose, bobcats, fox, deer and geese. Residents enjoy a variety of events throughout the year. There are three lakes in the area for swimming, fishing and canoeing.

  • Blueridge Estates

    The established country estate community of Blueridge Estate has opulent estate homes on 2-acre lots. If you're looking for a good deal on old-time acreage homes and want a slice of rural life, this subdivision should be on your list.

  • Bearspaw Village

    Just minutes from Calgary, Bearspaw Village has mature estate homes nestled among trees and rolling hills. It is home to rich fauna, flora and wildlife,

  • Watermark at Bearspaw

    Located off Twelve Mile Coulee Road, this is Bearspaw's newest subdivision with lot sizes averaging 1/4 to 1 acre. It has a Central Plaza featuring a pavilion with an outdoor kitchen, fire pit, barbecues, and a grass field for sports and events. There's an NBA-sized basketball court, three community playgrounds and more than 5km of paved walking and bicycling trails. A number of established home builders are constructing homes here.

Builder/Developer History

A number of developers have built homes in Bearspaw. They include WestRidge, JayWest and New West Luxury Homes.

FAQs about Bearspaw Real Estate and Neighborhood

  1. What kind of sports teams are in the area?

    Bearspaw is part of the Cochrane Minor Hockey Association (CMHA), which administers a minor hockey program for residents. The program is intended for players in the 5-18 age group. The leagues their teams play in include U18AA - Alberta Midget Hockey League, Alberta Minor Midget Hockey League, Alberta Major Bantam Hockey League and others.

  2. Will the kids have access to extracurricular activities?

    Absolutely! Bearspaw has parks, sports programs, an ice skating rink, a sports club and a minor hockey program. The Bow River is safe enough to go rafting or floating with your kids or grandkids. Tiny tots and young adults have numerous opportunities to enjoy an active lifestyle, pursue their favourite sports and connect with peers.

  3. Where do I get groceries?

    At the nearby Rocky Ridge Co-Op! You can also pick up fresh vegetables and meat from the Bearspaw Lifestyle Centre.

  4. Are there any cultural amenities?

    Art Hive is the community studio at Bearspaw Lifestyle Centre and a welcoming place for creating art. It is open to adults and kids.
