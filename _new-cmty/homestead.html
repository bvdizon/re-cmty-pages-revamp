Homestead Calgary Real Estate and Homes for Sale

When you picture Homestead, think prairie skies, mountain views, fields of wheat, breath-taking sceneries, hiking and walking trails, and a community full of amenities. With so much natural beauty around you, you can forget you are so close to the city.

Plant your roots in Homestead, a place that residents can feel proud to call home and will for many years to come. Diversity is celebrated and embraced, where neighbours feel connected to each other and the community. Discover Calgary's new northeast, with easy access to major roadways like Stoney Trail and Metis Trail.

Homestead Real Estate — a brand-new community for young single or married professionals and growing families.

There’s nothing quite as satisfying as buying a new home – especially if it is your first home. Whether you are recently married, have a young and growing family, or even a single executive working in Calgary, your home is where your heart is. Buying a home is fantastic, but what about buying a brand-new home in a brand-new community? You get to experience living in a completely new neighbourhood. With all your neighbours made up of young families like yours, going through the same excitement of being the first homeowners of that community.

Welcome to Homestead – a brand-new community for young single or married professionals and growing families. Here is your chance to buy a brand-new luxury home built by some of Canada’s best builders. Find a home that suits your family’s size, tastes, and requirements. Be a part of a community you will be proud to call your own.

Homestead is an up-and-coming neighbourhood in the northeast quadrant of Calgary, waiting to be discovered and inhabited by young and growing families and professionals. The prairie fields that cover miles and miles of land on one side of the community inspire the look and feel of this community. The Rocky-Mountain horizon on the other side guides you into the city. Homestead is the perfect amalgamation of city and rural life. Over here, you will find everything you need to lead a comfortable modern life and a peaceful surrounding that very few communities offer.

The community of Homestead lies to the east of Stoney Trail NE. On the opposite side of Stoney Trail NE are the neighbourhoods of Saddle Ridge, Taradale, and Coral Springs. As a resident of Homestead, you will have access to all the comforts that Homestead has to offer, plus access to the amenities of these neighbouring communities.

Homestead is close to Calgary International Airport, the Genesis Centre, and the Cross Iron commercial district. It’s a stone’s throw from various services and stores, Stoney Trail, and the 80th Avenue flyover.

Here’s a look at what the community will have to offer you in terms of various amenities and more.

Amenities in Homestead — Live, Work, & Play

Golf —

GTR Golf is an indoor golf course on 32 St NE that allows you to pursue your passion for golfing even during winter. GTR Golf software gives players a chance to practice their golfing skills with ultra-high-speed camera sensors and over 99% accuracy spin sensors.

For those who love going the traditional route on soft well-maintained rolling planes under blue skies, Wingfield Golf Club is located adjacent to Calgary International Airport. It provides golf lovers with a 27-hole layout that ranges from 5,300 to more than 7,200 yards of play. This club will soon take over as one of Calgary’s premier public golf clubs under new ownership.

Mountains —

The Rocky Mountains form a picturesque background as you drive into the city from your Homestead home. It’s a quick drive to the foothills as well. Homestead homes also offer owners the benefit of a picturesque view of the mountains from their homes.

Parks —

Homestead will be home to wide-open spaces and parks. The development is designed to include over 19 acres of existing wetlands this side of Stoney Trail. There are plans to make pathways, parks, four massive, 1-acre playgrounds, soccer fields, sheltered pavilions, basketball, and pickleball courts. Just now, a cricket pitch has been set up for the summer.

Nose Hill, lies in the northwest quadrant and extends over 11 square kilometres. The park features hiking trails for hiking lovers and off-leash areas for your pets. Plus, the area is teeming with wildlife and plant life.

Prairies Winds Park on Castleridge Blvd. NE is a significant destination park spanning 16 hectares and a fun place for the whole family. The park features outdoor skating rinks, pathways, a wading pool, a spray park, picnic shelter, basketball courts, cricket pitches, tennis courts, playscape, and more.

Shopping —

When it comes to shopping, residents of Homestead have a wide variety of shopping destinations that are a short drive away in the neighbouring communities. Sundridge Mall (approximately a 17-minute drive) offers fantastic dining experiences. If you are looking for big-box stores, East Hills Shopping Centre (about a 15-minute drive) is your go-to mall. CrossIron Mills offers you one of the best shopping experiences in the country. Spread over 1.4 million square feet, you can shop till you drop. For a one-stop shopping experience, Marlborough Mall (approximately an 18-minute drive) is the place to visit.

Fitness —

Coral Springs Park on Coral Keys Drive NE is fitted with various equipment so that you can access fitness in the gorgeous outdoors. Plus, there are multiple gyms and fitness centres nearby.

Restaurants and bars —

When it comes to eating – whether it is fine dining or picking up a pizza on a weeknight, there are a wide variety of restaurants just across Stoney Trail NE. From Indian cuisine at Amritsari Tadka restaurant and Spicy Root Restaurant to Chinese cuisine at Rasoi (which also serves East Indian cuisine), Taradale has a lot of exciting tastes for your taste buds.

For Pizzas, try Lava Pizza YYC on Saddlestone Drive. Pizza Master Fusion offers fantastic original flavours and some excellent cheese. The restaurant has dine-in, takeaway, and delivery as well.

Interesting Facts about Homestead

Schools In or Near Homestead

The children of Homestead are currently served by several neighbouring schools – public, private, and catholic. Now, there are plans to build two schools within the community.

Housing

Homestead will be comprised of single-family detached homes, attached homes, and townhomes. The neighbourhood will include almost 2,000 single-family homes (both laned and front garage homes) and multi-family homes when completed.

Developer/Builder History

The community of Homestead is currently being built by a group of builders comprising Genesis Builders, Partners Development Group, Excel Homes, and Trico Homes.

FAQs about Homestead Real Estate and Neighborhood

  1. Where do I get groceries?

    When a new area is being developed, residents often have to depend on the neighbouring communities for their groceries, etc. Homestead’s residents will have access to some of Calgary’s best grocery-shopping destinations.

    Asian Food Store is less than a 15-minute drive and in the community of Saddle Ridge.

    CHALO FreshCo Saddletowne is an all-in-one grocery store that is incredibly fantastic for South Asian ingredients.

    Fruiticana is an Indian grocery store on Saddletowne Cir NE.

    Sanjha Punjab is a major Indian grocery store on 64 Ave NE that is always stocked with everything you might need on a busy weeknight. The staff here are friendly.

    Real Canadian Superstore is about a 12-minute drive along McKnight Blvd NE, Castleridge. The store offers in-store shopping and curbside pickup, and you will even have the option of getting your groceries delivered to you.

  2. How far is Homestead from the airport?

    Calgary International Airport, or YYC as more commonly referred to, is just a 19-minute drive via 96 Avenue NE/Airport Trl NE W. Distance-wise, it is approximately 15.5 km to the airport.

    If you take the route via 100 St NE/Range Rd 285 and 96 Avenue NE/Airport Trl NE W, it will take about 21 minutes (17.2 km) to get to the airport.

    Via AB-201 N, the distance is 28.6 km or 23 minutes.

  3. Which is the closest hospital?

    In the city's northeast quadrant, Peter Lougheed Centre (PLC) is a 15-minute drive from Homestead. Once a small facility linked to the Calgary General Hospital, today it is a massive, 600-bed hospital. It is spread over 506,000 square feet, providing a range of healthcare facilities and specialties like limb preservation clinics, pediatrics, complex disease management, mental health and addiction services, and vascular surgery.

  4. Which is the nearest Rec Centre?

    Village Square Leisure Centre is 5.5 acres of sheer fun for the entire family. This indoor leisure centre is located in the community of Pineridge and is just a 15-minute drive from Homestead. The rec centre boasts a fantastic indoor water park with waterslides, a wave pool, steam room, rope swings, and a dive tank. There is a children’s area with swinging bridges, spray guns, and dump buckets. The facility also has a gymnasium, fitness and dance studios, skating, weight rooms, rock climbing wall, and more.

    Genesis Centre on Falconridge Blvd. NE is another rec centre for drop-in sports and to take part in a wide variety of community events.

  5. Will I feel safe in this community? What is the crime like here?

    Yes, you will feel safe here. Residents who currently stay in Homestead believe they are much safer here than in many other neighbourhoods.
